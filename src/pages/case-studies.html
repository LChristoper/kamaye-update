April 2021

Chocolatos Drink Powder

Objective

Challenge:

Garudafood faced declining awareness and sales of its key product line, "Chocolatos," exacerbated by global economic declines and shifts in consumer behavior towards digital platforms, rendering traditional marketing methods ineffective.

Solution:

Garudafood launched the "Chocolatos Recipe" campaign to target Millennials and Gen Z. This monthly series of cooking campaigns collaborated with influencers across various demographics and expertise levels to showcase Chocolatos as a versatile ingredient for homemade desserts. Utilizing TikTok's popularity and the trend of home cooking, the campaign produced viral content, achieving over 5 million views monthly at a low cost per view (CPV). The campaign's success increased Chocolatos' regional sales and market visibility, with TikTok influencers gaining significant social media prominence. This initiative effectively bridged traditional marketing with the digital landscape, maintaining relevance and awareness during the pandemic while establishing Chocolatos as a popular choice for homemade treats.

June 2022

Line Webtoon

Objective

Challenge:

The primary challenge was to expand Naver Webtoon's audience in Indonesia, transitioning from appealing mainly to young millennials to effectively engaging Gen Z, who have significant influence in the digital entertainment space.

Solution:

To address this challenge, Naver Webtoon partnered with Kamaye, an influencer agency, to execute a multifaceted marketing campaign. This campaign was strategically launched during Ramadan to maximize cultural relevance. It leveraged TikTok as the main platform, involving 87 influencers to enhance conversions, engagement, and awareness among Gen Z users. The influencers were categorized based on their strengths and employed various innovative concepts such as Virtual Casting, Fun Dubbing Comedies, Story Reviews, and Song Covers. This diverse strategy aimed to maximize audience engagement, particularly focusing on promoting "Operation True Love," a new title. The campaign achieved over 13 million views, 335,000 comments, and reached more than 13 million unique users, with Story Reviews driving the highest engagement
